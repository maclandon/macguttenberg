Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Marissa Joy Wellness ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide when visiting marissajoywellness.com.

Information we collect

When you submit the contact form on this website, we collect your name, email address, and any information you choose to share in your message. We do not collect any other personal information automatically, and we do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

How we use your information

The information you provide through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

This website uses Formspree to process contact form submissions. When you submit the form, your information is transmitted to Formspree's servers. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io.

This website uses Google Fonts to serve typefaces. Google may collect limited data as part of this service. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com.

Data retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us directly.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at marissa@marissajoywellness.com.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at marissa@marissajoywellness.com.
