Case Study

MG Tax Inc. — From paper to fully digital in under 30 days.

Before
After

The situation: Mark had been running his tax practice since 1994. Every client interaction was paper-based. Payments were cash only. There was no way to work remotely, share documents, or scale without adding more physical space and more people.

What I built: A complete digital infrastructure — cloud-based document management, digital intake forms with e-signatures, online payment processing, a client portal for document uploads and status tracking, and video conferencing for remote meetings.

What happened: The practice went from fully paper-based to 100% digital in weeks. Admin time dropped by 50%. Clients could now onboard, pay, and communicate without ever coming into the office. The business didn't just survive — it grew.

Download Designs by AND — From zero to profitable Etsy shop in 8 months.

The Storefront
The Product
Ashley DeMaria
Founder, Download Designs by AND

The situation: Ashley had a creative vision and design talent but no brand, no shop, and no idea how to turn it into a real business. She needed someone to build the full foundation from scratch.

What I built: Complete brand identity (logo, color palette, typography), Etsy shop setup and optimization, product listing strategy, SEO for Etsy search, pricing calculator to ensure profitability, social media launch plan, and customer engagement guidance.

What happened: The shop launched and hit profitability within its first full month. By month 8, Ashley had completed 74 orders, generated $2,839 in revenue, and reached a 52% profit margin — with sales growing from 8 orders in month 1 to 15 in month 8. She earned a 4.8-star rating on Etsy.
