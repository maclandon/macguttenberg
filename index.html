About Mac

I got into this by fixing what wasn't working.

My first project was a tax practice doing $500K a year — running entirely on paper, cash, and good intentions. No systems, no digital presence, no way to scale. Just decades of doing things the hard way.

I rebuilt everything from scratch. Digital intake, online payments, a client portal, automated workflows. The operation ran better within weeks than it had in years.

Every small business has this gap. The product is real, the work ethic is there, but the infrastructure is always the last thing that gets built. I'm based in New York City and work with businesses remotely across the country. I don't just consult — I get in, understand your situation, and build something that works.
